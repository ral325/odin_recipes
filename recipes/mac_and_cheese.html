Mac and Cheese

Boxed Mac and Cheese: boil water, add pasta, drain, add milk, add cheese powder, mix and serve

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Annie's
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta, cook for 8 minutes
  3. Drain
  4. Add milk to pot, warm briefly, then add powdered cheese. Mix.
  5. Add cooked pasta, mix and serve
